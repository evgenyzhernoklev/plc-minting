How Does Minting Go?

After formalizing rent with PLATINCOIN you will get patented dedicated minting equipment with a unique User ID. The company will provide the equipment for rent at its own data centres and connect it to the system. A certain initial amount of coins is required for minting – and it is included in the rented package. That’s why you will start getting new coins right after the getting connected to the system.

  • Controller

    Dashboard, User Wallet, Exchange Speed Storage

  • Node

  • Hard Wallet

The rent is concluded for 36 months and during this period minting will provide you with coins every day and put them in your wallet.

The Мax Rate indicator will calculate the output and speed of coin generation. The calculations are based on the current index of the system per month. Minted coins will be credited to your wallet every day.

The minting equipment set includes:

  • Your unique User ID;
  • Detailed separate node of the blockchain;
  • Fully pre-installed electronic wallet with a complete set of rented coins for successful minting;
  • Controller to monitor the automated and correct crediting of coins generated in minting.
